BuildingBridges

Building Bridges is involved in nearly 1500 communities across more than 25 countries and territories worldwide. We are focused on helping individuals whose homes have been ravaged by war and natural disasters. We envision a world where children and their families are not homeless,hungry,uneducated,sick etc because of the adverse effects of wars and natural disasters.
